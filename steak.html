Description :

Steak is a flavorful cut of beef that is typically grilled or pan-seared to your desired level of doneness.

Ingredients :

  • Steak (ribeye, sirloin, filet mignon, etc.)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Garlic cloves (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (like rosemary or thyme) (optional)

Instructions :

  1. Take the steak out of the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature for about 30 minutes before cooking. This helps ensure even cooking.
  2. Preheat your grill or skillet to high heat.
  3. Pat the steak dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. This helps achieve a good sear.
  4. Season both sides of the steak generously with salt and black pepper. You can also add other seasonings if desired.
  5. If using a skillet, add a small amount of olive oil or butter to the pan and let it heat up until shimmering.
  6. Place the steak on the hot grill or skillet. For a medium-rare steak, cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, depending on the thickness of the steak. Adjust the cooking time for your preferred level of doneness.
  7. If desired, during the last minute of cooking, you can add crushed garlic cloves and fresh herbs to the pan for extra flavor. Baste the steak with the melted butter or oil.
  8. Once cooked to your liking, remove the steak from the heat and let it rest for about 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat.
  9. Slice the steak against the grain and serve. Enjoy your perfectly cooked steak!

Description :

Spaghetti is a popular Italian pasta dish made from wheat and water, often served with various sauces and toppings.

Ingredients :

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sauce of your choice (e.g., marinara, Alfredo, pesto)
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Grated cheese (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions :

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add a generous amount of salt to the boiling water.
  2. Add the spaghetti pasta to the boiling water. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente (usually around 8-12 minutes).
  4. Once cooked, drain the pasta in a colander. You can reserve a cup of pasta water if you plan to use it in your sauce.
  5. If desired, toss the drained pasta with a little olive oil to prevent sticking.
  6. Serve the spaghetti with your choice of sauce and toppings, such as grated cheese or fresh herbs.
  7. Enjoy your spaghetti!

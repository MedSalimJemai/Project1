Description :

The chicken is roasted slowly in the oven, with golden crispy skin and tender meat inside.

Ingredients :

  • Whole chicken
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic cloves
  • Fresh herbs (like rosemary, thyme, or parsley)
  • Lemon
  • Onion
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Potatoes (optional)

Instructions :

  1. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. Prepare the chicken by removing any giblets from the cavity and patting it dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub the chicken all over with olive oil or melted butter. Season generously with salt and black pepper, both inside and out.
  4. Stuff the cavity with crushed garlic cloves, fresh herbs, and lemon halves.
  5. If desired, place chopped onions, carrots, and potatoes around the chicken in the roasting pan.
  6. Truss the chicken by tying the legs together with kitchen twine and tucking the wing tips under the body.
  7. Place the chicken breast-side up on a rack in a roasting pan.
  8. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) when measured at the thickest part of the thigh.
  9. Baste the chicken occasionally with the pan juices to keep it moist.
  10. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat.
  11. Carve and serve the roasted chicken with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
